Exploring Spatial

Welcome to my playground for exploring the world of web development, maps, and GIS (Global Information Systems).

Introduction

The main purpose of this site is to host labs from my blog, Exploring Spatial (see "Recent Posts," on left). My blog is just a personal technology journal where I document what I learn as I explore new technology my job. I've found that documenting what I learn in my blog is a good learning aid. Making my journal public forces me to dig in a little deeper and be more thorough. I closed my S-Corporation, Byteworks, in 2010 when I joined Garmin International, but I resurrected my ByteworksInc.com CSS template for my Exploring Spatial site. I kept the Byteworks logo just because I like it. I'm kind of nostalgic that way.

The site is built as a single-page Backbone application. Most of the interesting code may contained Backbone Views stored under /js/views/demos. Feel free to browse the code, reuse what you want, or suggest better ways of implementing a demo.
