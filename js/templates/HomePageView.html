Exploring Spatial

The main purpose of this site is to host labs/demos for my blog, Exploring Spatial. My blog is where I document what I learn as I explore new technology for my job. It's my personal, technical journal that that I share publically. Writing about what I discover is a learning aid, and putting it on the Web forces me to dig in a little deeper than I might have otherwise.

I resurrected this site layout from my former S-Corp, Byteworks, Inc, which dissolved in 2010 when I went to work for Garmin. The Byteworks logo is still on the site for nostalgic reasons.

This site is built as a single-page Backbone application. Most of the interesting code is contained in Backbone Views stored under /js/views/demos. Feel free to browse the code, reuse what you want, or suggest better ways of implementing a demo.
